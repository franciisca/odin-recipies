Piscola

Piscola is a sweet but strong drink, usually drank at parties and social gatherings in Chile, South America.

It is super easy to make!

Ingredients for one glass:

  • A 200 ml glass.
  • 80 ml of Pisco liquor.
  • A cola drink of your choice.
  • Ice.

Preparation:

  1. Fill half of the glass with ice.
  2. Pour the 80 ml of Pisco.
  3. Fill the rest of the glass with cola.

Enjoy!
